Problems facing Agriculture in Rwanda

Let's save our country

Soil Erosion

This is soil erosion which could cause farmers to lose more money in thier job of farming. Soil erosion means a gradual process that occurs when the impact of water or wind detaches and removes soil particles, causing the soil to deteriorate. This could be caused by:

  • hard rain
  • flowing water

Lack of Capital

This is the problem of lack of capital in Rwanda which could cause economic problems. Lack of capital means not having to cover overhead expenses. This could be many reasons for example:

  • lack of saving structures
  • excessive costs
  • devloping capital

Pests and Diseases

This is the problem of pests and diseases which could reproduce virus or infectious disease on the fruits which basically means it is not edible.
The meaning of pests and diseases are insects for example:

  • slug
  • snail
  • nematode
